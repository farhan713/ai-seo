# Use cases — Growth plan (₹899/mo)

Growth includes **everything in Starter**, plus **blogs**, **backlinks**, **full content calendar**, **multi-platform social packs**, **GA4**, and **monthly executive email** opt-in.

---

## Primary journeys

1. **Content engine**  
   Use **Blogs** (AI drafts from presets) → generate **social post pack** on a blog with **LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook** captions.

2. **Authority & off-page**  
   Track **Backlinks** checklist alongside audits and keywords.

3. **Full calendar**  
   **Content calendar** with a high item cap; seed from audit **content pillar** ideas.

4. **Web analytics**  
   **Web analytics (GA4)** → Connect Google → enter **Property ID** (from GA4 Admin) → **Sync** for sessions, users, top pages.

5. **Reporting**  
   Opt in to **monthly executive email** on **Business profile** (requires Resend on server).  
   Use **Share report** for ad-hoc read-only links.

6. **Competitors**  
   Up to **3** competitor watch URLs with refresh/diff snapshots.

---

## Not included on Growth

- **Social ads** page and automated Meta posting (Elite)  
- **Ad creative angles** block in Site audit (Elite)  
- Monthly email **PDF** attachment (Elite)

---

Same as Starter: **Gemini**, **PageSpeed**, **local SEO pack** toggle under **Business profile**. GA4 uses Google OAuth, not a pasted API key.
