AI SEO TOOL — USER GUIDE (ELITE PLAN, ₹1,599/MO)
=================================================

Elite is Growth plus paid social depth: a Social ads workspace, ad creative
angles inside Site audit, one-click “campaign idea → draft,” and monthly email
with PDF when your operator enables it. Read User-Guide-Starter.txt and
User-Guide-Growth.txt for shared screens; this file focuses on Elite-only parts.


WHAT MAKES ELITE USEFUL
-----------------------
• Audit and paid ads together: “Ad creative angles” (Meta-style and Google RSA-style
  blocks) generated in the same audit that fixes SEO.
• Social ads hub: store Meta credentials (JSON), view AI drafts with captions and
  images (stock or Imagen when the server allows).
• Campaign ideas → Create draft: second AI pass saves today’s row in Social ads
  (same daily slot as admin-generated drafts).
• Highest limits: 100 keywords, 5 competitors, full Growth feature set.


ELITE LIMITS
------------
• Tracked keywords: up to 100.
• Competitors: up to 5 URLs.


SITE AUDIT — AD CREATIVE ANGLES
-------------------------------
What it does: after normal audit sections, Elite may show Ad creative angles with
headline/body variants for Meta and Google RSA-style formats.

How: copy lines into Ads Manager; respect character limits in the ad editor.

Benefit: organic fixes and paid creative stay aligned.

Not on Starter or Growth.


SOCIAL ADS
----------
What it does:
1. Credentials JSON — long-lived Facebook Page token and Instagram Business Account ID
   (? tooltip outlines the token workflow).
2. Recent drafts — daily SocialAd rows: caption pack (Instagram + Facebook sections),
   optional AI image or stock fallback.

How: get tokens via Meta Business Suite / Graph API (production needs proper app
review — operator’s job) → paste JSON → Save → review drafts → copy to publish manually.

Benefit: one place for creative and visuals tied to your brand.

Growth clients do not see this page.


CAMPAIGN IDEAS — CREATE DRAFT IN SOCIAL ADS
-------------------------------------------
What it does: on Campaign ideas, each card can run “Create draft in Social ads.” That:
1. Builds a brief from the idea.
2. Calls the AI with the same social ad copy stack used elsewhere.
3. Updates the row for today’s date in Social ads (caption + image like the admin path).

How: generate ideas → pick the best → Create draft → open Social ads to copy/publish.

Note: one draft slot per day — a new Create draft replaces today’s row (same as admin “today’s draft”).

Elite-only; Growth sees ideas without this button.


MONTHLY EMAIL — PDF (ELITE)
---------------------------
When Resend, cron, and Elite PDF generation are configured, the monthly email can
include a PDF attachment (Growth gets HTML only). Opt in on Business profile like Growth.


FULL ELITE CHECKLIST (TRY ONCE)
-------------------------------
1. Business profile, optional BYOK keys, local SEO toggle.
2. Site audit — confirm Ad creative angles section.
3. Keywords (up to 100), competitors (up to 5), calendar, blogs, backlinks, GA4 sync.
4. Campaign ideas with custom direction → Create draft → check Social ads.
5. Share report link for a stakeholder.
6. Save Meta credentials and review latest draft image/caption.


TROUBLESHOOTING (ELITE)
-----------------------
• No ad angles: run a new audit; feature is gated to Elite in the API.
• Create draft fails: Gemini (server or BYOK); confirm Elite plan active.
• No Imagen image: server Gemini + Imagen; failures may fall back to stock art.
• Posting not automatic: MVP may still expect you to copy/paste into Meta.
