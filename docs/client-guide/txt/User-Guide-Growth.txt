AI SEO TOOL — USER GUIDE (GROWTH PLAN, ₹899/MO)
===============================================

Growth includes everything in Starter, plus blogs, backlinks, GA4 analytics,
campaign ideas, higher limits, three-platform social packs, and the option to
opt in to a monthly executive email. Read User-Guide-Starter.txt first; this
file only adds what is new or different on Growth.


WHAT MAKES GROWTH USEFUL
------------------------
• Content engine: AI blog drafts plus LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook captions
  from blogs and audits.
• Measurement: Google Analytics 4 next to Search Console (sessions, users, top pages).
• Scale: up to 50 keywords, 500 calendar items, 3 competitors.
• Campaign ideas with optional free-text direction — but no “create draft in
  Social ads” button; that is Elite only.


SAME AS STARTER, BUT HIGHER LIMITS
------------------------------------
These screens work like Starter unless noted:

• Keywords: up to 50 phrases.
• Content calendar: up to 500 items.
• Competitors: up to 3 URLs.
• Site audit, crawl, Search Console, checklist, share report: same idea.

Social pack from audit: all three platforms (LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook).


BLOGS
-----
What it does: long-form AI blog drafts using your business profile (vertical, goal, industry).

How: open Blogs → create or open a draft → generate where the UI allows → edit in your CMS.

Benefit: faster first drafts that match your positioning.

Starter has no blog generator. Tip: after a post is ready, use Social post pack on the blog for three platforms.


BACKLINKS
---------
What it does: checklist of backlink/directory-style tasks (your operator sets defaults).

How: open Backlinks, work items, mark status as you submit or verify.

Benefit: off-page work stays organized instead of random bookmarks.


WEB ANALYTICS (GA4)
-------------------
What it does: Google OAuth (same app as Search Console, extra redirect and Analytics
Data API). You enter your numeric GA4 Property ID, then Sync for recent totals and top pages.

How:
1. Connect Google (read-only analytics scope).
2. In GA4: Admin → Property settings → copy Property ID.
3. Paste in the app, save property, Sync now.

Benefit: see traffic next to SEO queries.

Starter users are sent away from this page. The ? tooltip by Property ID explains where to find the ID.


CAMPAIGN IDEAS
--------------
What it does: optional “Your direction” text (up to 2,000 characters) plus one click
sends your profile and today’s date context to the AI. You get four ideas: hook,
format, rationale, trend/occasion, hashtags.

Important: the model does not browse live news. Verify holidays and events before spending ad money.

Benefit: breaks creative block with timely, business-specific angles.

Growth vs Elite: on Growth you only see ideas. Elite adds “Create draft in Social ads.”


MONTHLY EXECUTIVE EMAIL
-----------------------
If your operator enabled Resend and cron, you can opt in on Business profile to a
monthly HTML summary email.

Elite may add a PDF attachment when the server is configured for it.


QUICK COMPARISON: GROWTH VS STARTER
-----------------------------------
• Blogs: no on Starter, yes on Growth.
• Backlinks: no on Starter, yes on Growth.
• GA4: no on Starter, yes on Growth.
• Campaign ideas: no on Starter, yes on Growth (ideas only).
• Calendar: 2 items Starter, 500 Growth.
• Keywords: 10 Starter, 50 Growth.
• Competitors: 1 Starter, 3 Growth.
• Social pack: LinkedIn only Starter, three platforms Growth.
• Monthly email opt-in: hidden Starter, visible Growth.


TROUBLESHOOTING (GROWTH)
------------------------
• GA4 “not configured”: operator must add GA4 callback URI and enable Analytics Data API.
• Campaign ideas error: Gemini key on server or BYOK.
• No “Create draft” on ideas: normal on Growth — upgrade to Elite for that.
