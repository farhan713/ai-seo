AI SEO TOOL — USER GUIDE (STARTER PLAN, ₹499/MO)
================================================

This guide explains every Starter feature in plain language: what it is, where
to find it, how to use it, what you gain, and what you do not get on this tier.


WHAT MAKES STARTER USEFUL
-------------------------
• Lowest-cost way to get AI site audits, Search Console alignment, keyword
  tracking, and a small content calendar.
• One competitor URL and two calendar slots keep scope focused; upgrade to
  Growth when you need blogs, GA4, and more volume.
• Social captions from audits are LinkedIn-only on Starter. Growth adds
  Instagram and Facebook on blogs and audits.


BEFORE YOU START
----------------
1. Register and log in — you land on Overview.
2. Open Business profile (dashboard settings). Fill in:
   • Business name, website, description — used everywhere the AI writes for you.
   • Industry vertical and marketing goal — tune audits to your business type.
   • Target keywords — feed the keyword tracker and prompts.
   • Internal links (JSON) — optional list of important URLs for linking hints.
3. Optional: add your own Gemini and PageSpeed API keys on the same page (? tooltips
   explain how). If you skip them, the server uses its keys when configured.
4. Optional: turn on Local SEO if you want Google Business Profile–style ideas
   in audits (works best for local businesses).

Why this matters: your first audit and suggestions will match your business
instead of sounding generic.


OVERVIEW (DASHBOARD HOME)
-------------------------
• Growth score appears when you have audit data — one number with a short
  explanation of what changed.
• Audit progress appears when you have two or more audits — compares early vs
  latest technical signals.

Use it: check weekly after new audits. Value: see “are we moving?” without spreadsheets.


BUSINESS PROFILE
----------------
Same as “Before you start.” Every AI feature uses this context.

Starter note: you will not see the monthly executive email opt-in (Growth/Elite only).


SITE AUDIT
----------
What it does: loads your page, runs technical checks (including Lighthouse when
keys exist), and asks the AI for structured help: keywords, meta, CTAs, FAQs,
lead ideas, a social caption pack (LinkedIn only on Starter), and more.

How: confirm website URL in profile → run the audit → copy sections into your CMS.

Benefit: actionable, brand-specific output in minutes.

Compared to other plans: Growth gets three platforms in the social pack from the
same audit. Elite adds “ad creative angles” (paid ads copy) — not on Starter.


SITE CRAWL
----------
What it does: follows links from your start URL (within app limits) and reports
structural/technical notes.

How: start a crawl; read results when finished.

Benefit: catches issues beyond a single-page audit.


SEARCH PERFORMANCE (GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE)
------------------------------------------
What it does: connects your Google account (OAuth) and syncs recent search query data.

How: Connect Google → Sync when you want fresh data → review queries and clicks.

Benefit: see what people actually search before you rewrite titles and content.

Requires: your operator must configure Google OAuth on the server.


KEYWORDS
--------
What it does: tracks phrases you care about; can line up with Search Console when connected.

How: add phrases; export CSV if the app offers it.

Starter limit: 10 tracked keywords maximum.

Benefit: one priority list tied to real data when GSC is on.


CONTENT CALENDAR
----------------
What it does: plan topics and dates; can be filled from audit “content pillar” ideas.

How: add items, change status, delete when done.

Starter limit: 2 items total — a third is blocked.

Benefit: light planning without paying for Growth volume. Upgrade when you need a full calendar.


ON-PAGE CHECKLIST
-----------------
What it does: per-URL checklist (titles, headings, schema, etc.) with saved checkboxes.

How: enter URL → load checklist → tick items as you fix them.

Benefit: repeatable QA so pages do not slip backward.


COMPETITORS
-----------
What it does: save competitor URLs, take snapshots, refresh to see title/meta/H1 changes.

How: add one URL (Starter cap). Use Refresh after they change their site.

Benefit: quick competitive view without another tool.

Rules: Starter allows 1 competitor. You cannot add your own domain.


SHARE REPORT
------------
What it does: creates a time-limited public link (/r/...) anyone can open without
logging in — snapshot-style summary for stakeholders.

How: choose expiry → generate → copy link → revoke when done.

Benefit: share progress without sharing passwords.

Requires: production must set AUTH_URL or NEXT_PUBLIC_APP_URL so links are correct.


WHAT STARTER DOES NOT INCLUDE
-----------------------------
• AI Blogs — Growth and up.
• Backlinks checklist — Growth and up.
• Web analytics (GA4) — Growth and up.
• Campaign ideas — Growth and up.
• Social ads page and Meta drafts — Elite only.
• Ad creative angles inside site audit — Elite only.
• Monthly executive email opt-in — Growth and up (not shown on Starter).


TROUBLESHOOTING (STARTER)
-------------------------
• Audit AI errors: check server GEMINI_API_KEY or your Gemini key in Business profile.
• No Lighthouse scores: PageSpeed API key on server or your BYOK key.
• Search Console will not connect: OAuth redirect URI and API enabled in Google Cloud.
• Calendar will not add a third item: expected on Starter — delete one or upgrade.
